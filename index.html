I also hold strong feminist values that I try to fight for everyday but that doesn't mean it was my feminist duty to vote for a woman simply because she's a woman. I'm very conflicted because yes I'm Glad our government is predominantly republican again and I believe we will restore this nation to be a Godly nation but that does not mean we should persecute those who choose not to believe in our God nor harm our brothers and sisters who love differently than we do. Yes, We are a nation under God BUT we are also the land of the free and every American no matter the race, gender, or sexual preference deserves to live freely.
